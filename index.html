Dune Web Novel

Dune is a science fiction novel written by Frank Herbert, first published in 1965. It is considered one of the greatest science fiction novels of all time.

The story takes place in the distant future amidst a feudal interstellar society. It explores themes of politics, religion, and power, and follows the journey of young Paul Atreides as he navigates the dangerous sands of the desert planet Arrakis.

The novel has been adapted into various media, including films, television series, and video games. It has also influenced many other works in the science fiction genre.
